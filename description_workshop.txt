[h2]Overview[/h2] [img]https://i.imgur.com/jdunrUl.png[/img]
This mod adds a new and unique structure to your world, designed to be a thrilling risk-reward experience for brave explorers. Mysterious mechanical doors will be hidden underground, holding dangerous obstacle courses that push your combat and evasion skills to their limits.

Do you have what it takes to conquer the Challenge Vaults?

[h2]Compatibility[/h2] [img]https://i.imgur.com/jdunrUl.png[/img]
This mod has been carefully designed to ensure no mod conflicts are possible; EVERYTHING should be compatible, allowing it to be used and enjoyed in ANY modpack.

MULTIPLAYER IS NOT CURRENTLY SUPPORTED, but this is subject to change in the future!
